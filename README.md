# KEZMAN-SUPERSTORES-SALES-DASHBOARD-
This is a client project for Kezman Superstore. The goal was to analyse sales data and build an interactive dashboard to help management understand business performance and make data-driven decisions. 
The dashboard analyzes 5.11M in total sales and 20K units sold across 4 product categories and 4 regions.

![image alt](https://github.com/isaacayeni225/KEZMAN-SUPERSTORES-SALES-DASHBOARD-/blob/main/WORK1.png?raw=true)

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 5.11M 
- Total Quantity Sold: 20K 
- Units Top Category: Electronics - 1.83M in Sales, 7K Units, 35.15% of volume 
- Top Region: West - 1.35M in Sales 
- Trend: Electronics leads in every region. West + South have the highest overall sales

## Key Insights
- Revenue Driver: Electronics is the highest performing category, contributing the most to both sales and quantity.
- Regional Performance: The West region outperforms all other regions.
- Opportunity: Beauty has the lowest sales at 0.77M. Potential for marketing/promotion to grow this category.
- Consistent Demand: Clothing and Home categories show steady sales across all regions.
  
## Business Impact
This dashboard helps Kezman Superstore:
- Track revenue and sales volume in real time
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Spot underperforming categories for targeted action
- Support inventory and marketing decisions
